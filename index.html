Our Story

Authentic Flavors,
Timeless Traditions

Madam Tang is a newly opened pan-Asian snack cart located in the heart of Flushing’s Tangram. Inspired by cherished family recipes and the vibrant flavors of Southeast Asia, we bring a fresh, modern take on classic street-style snacks.

Each morning, our team prepares everything from scratch, using traditional techniques passed down through generations. From savory bites to sweet pastries, every item is crafted with care to share the comforting flavors of home with the community.
